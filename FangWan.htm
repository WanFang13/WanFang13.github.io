Biography

I am a postdoctor of computer science and technology in the School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences , advised by Prof. Qingming Huang. I got my Ph.D. and Master Degree in 2019 and 2016 respectively at PRISDL in the School of Electronic, Electrical and Communication Engineering, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, advised by Prof. Qixiang Ye.

My research interests include computer vision and machine learning, specifically for weakly supervised learning, feature learning, and visual object detection.
